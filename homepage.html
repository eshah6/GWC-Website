Girls Who Code

@ IMSA

Girls Who Code aims to close the gender gap in technological fields, specifically computer science, through project-based learning. By creating a supportive community of women and non-binary coders, we want to build confidence in those who have previously shied away from pursuing their interests in computer science. We hope for more passionate coders to work in teams to build projects of their interest while learning computer science concepts applicable to any project and building confidence to pursue a technological major.
