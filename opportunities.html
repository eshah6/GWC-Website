Opportunities to Get Involved with GWC!

General Member

General Members of Girls Who Code meet once a month on Mondays! Members are encouraged to participate in additional events and opportunities, including guest speaker panels, tutoring, and workshops. Being a general member provides an excellent opportunity to engage with Girls Who Code and expand your knowledge in computer science. Students can join Girls Who Code as a general member at any time during the school year. Our next General Assembly (GA) will be held on Monday, December 9th from 7:00-8:00 PM in the TV Pit. We hope to see you there!

Board

The Girls Who Code board oversees the club's events and creates opportunities for general members to engage with the organization. Our club currently consists of three teams: Events, Projects, and Education. For detailed information about each team's current initiatives, please visit the Teams Page.





Events

Girls Who Code hosts many events including workshops, volunteer opportunities, guest speaker panels, and more! Anyone interested can attend these events. For more information, refer to the Events Page!
