Events!

GWC x Hadron: Scavenger Hunt

STATUS: Past

Organized in collaboration with IMSA's STEM Newspaper, Hadron, set up a scavenger hunt prompting participants to identify and correct misinformation about influential, yet unknown women in STEM and Computer Science. The event offered pizza to participants and gift cards to winners of the scavenger hunt!

Sip & Study

STATUS: Past

Put together by Bella, the AP CSA and coding Sip & Study Sessions bring together coffee and computer science lovers to learn together. From April 8th, 2025 to May 1st, 2025 the sessions provided various forms of coffee and sweet treats!

APCSA Tutoring

STATUS: Upcoming

Mentored by Sam and Bella, APCSA features multiple workshops and resources available to IMSA students in preparation for the AP Computer Science A exam. Starting in November of 2024, weekly study sessions have been held alongside practice tests and fun hands-on activities.

GA Scavenger Hunt

STATUS: Past

Starting off the school year with a fun activity, GWC kickstarted their first GA with a scavenger hunt! Making participants decode different symbols around the main campus, the winning pair takes home a gift card. This event helped students all around IMSA come together and learn more about the Girls Who Code initiative.

Guest Speaker Panel

STATUS: Past

Tanvi and Nysa hosted their first guest speaker panel. Featuring female IMSA alums and women working in the computer science industry, it was amazing to hear their new perspectives of the tech industry today. Guest speakers came from institutions such as Carnegie Mellon and Urbana Champaign, featuring Phyllis Shen, Drshika Asher, Shaguni Kumar, and Anhiti.

Website Building

STATUS: Ongoing

Led by Tianqing, Eshika, and Sophia, website building was an activity part of GWC Projects Team! Under this division, members were able to partner up and code the official IMSA Girls Who Code website. Hosting weekly meetings and offering HTML resources, a group of ten girl coderd were able to create this website!
